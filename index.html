CS109 Final Project - Analyzing the payoff to public office

Welcome to 'The Committed Gits'

As part of our final project for CS109, we use machine-learning and data wrangling techniques to analyze the payoffs to public office in India.

Databases

We use two major data sources:

  • www.myneta.info/: We use the data for the Lok Sabha elections in India for 2004, 2009, and 2014
  • Prowess: We use firm level data to see if firms in which members of parliament hold shares do better than others on average

Goals

There are three major question we would like to answer:

  • To what extent can criminal history (type and number of cases) be used to predict victory?
  • What is the level of asset accumulation for a member of parliament while in office?
  • Do firms that are partly owned by members of parliament do better than others?
